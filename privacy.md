# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

AskFortis is a conversational assistant available through WhatsApp. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with the AskFortis chatbot.

## Information We Collect

When users interact with AskFortis through WhatsApp, the following information may be received:

- WhatsApp phone number
- Messages sent to the chatbot
- Technical metadata provided by WhatsApp (timestamps, message identifiers)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

## How We Use Information

Information received from users is used only for:

- Generating responses to user messages
- Improving the functionality of the chatbot
- Ensuring system security and preventing abuse

## AI Processing

Messages sent to AskFortis may be processed by artificial intelligence services to generate responses. These services may include third-party providers such as OpenAI.

User messages may therefore be transmitted to external AI systems for processing.

## Data Storage

AskFortis does not permanently store personal user data unless necessary for system operation, debugging, or security purposes.

## Data Sharing

User data is not sold or shared with third parties except:

- when required to operate the AI response system
- when required by law

## Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are implemented to protect the system from unauthorized access or misuse.
